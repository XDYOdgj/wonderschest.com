Tonffi 4FT Plug-in LED Ceiling Light

This modern and versatile ceiling light is designed for both indoor and outdoor use, offering a powerful 36W LED solution with a bright 4000LM output. It delivers consistent, flicker-free illumination across all modes, eliminating eye strain and ensuring a safe, comfortable environment. The 5000K cool white color enhances visibility and gives your space a sleek, contemporary look. Whether you're lighting a workspace or a living area, this light is an efficient and stylish choice.

With a plug-and-play design, installation is quick and hassle-free. Just hang the light using the included carabiner and connect it to any standard power outlet. Its IP65 rating makes it perfect for use in wet or dusty environments, including bathrooms, kitchens, and basements. The anti-corrosion and anti-mosquito features add to its durability, making it ideal for rooms with high moisture levels.

Key Features

  • Plug and Play Design: Easy to install with a carabiner and included power cord, perfect for immediate use in any indoor or outdoor setting.
  • Super Bright 36W LED: Provides 4000LM of energy-efficient, flicker-free light with a cool white color that enhances visibility.
  • Linkable Tube: Connect up to 10 LED lights for customizable setups in large spaces like garages, warehouses, or basements.
  • Upgraded LED Technology: Offers bright and soft illumination without blinding the eyes, with a lifespan of up to 30,000 hours and durable PC housing.
  • IP65 Waterproof & Dustproof: Ideal for wet areas such as bathrooms, kitchens, and basements, with anti-corrosion and anti-mosquito protection.

Whether you're lighting a workbench, garage, or boutique, this Tonffi LED light bar provides reliable illumination with a versatile setup. The flexibility to choose between plug-in and wired installation makes it adaptable for a range of environments. For those looking for an efficient, long-lasting, and stylish lighting solution, this product is the perfect fit.
