Stylish, Dimmable Wooden Handle Pendant Light

Elevate your home's ambiance with this elegant dimmable wooden handle pendant light. Paired with a classic macaron white iron shade, it creates a timeless aesthetic that complements modern, rustic, and transitional decor effortlessly.
