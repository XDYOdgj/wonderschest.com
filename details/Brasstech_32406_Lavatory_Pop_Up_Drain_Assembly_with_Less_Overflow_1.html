Brasstech 324/06 Lavatory Pop-Up Drain Assembly

Enhance your bathroom with a reliable and stylish drainage solution. This pop-up drain is specially engineered for glass sinks, ensuring optimal performance and a seamless look.

Why Choose Brasstech 324/06?

Designed to deliver anti-overflow protection, this drain assembly prevents water buildup underneath the sink, keeping your bathroom environment clean and hygienic. Its antique brass finish adds a touch of elegance that complements traditional bathroom designs.

With a completely polished surface, it is built to be easy to clean and maintain, making it ideal for both modern and classic setups. The universal fit ensures it works with most standard 1-1/2-inch sink openings, providing versatility for a variety of applications.

Key Features

  • Antique Brass finish: Classic style that enhances your bathroom's overall aesthetic.

  • Precision-engineered: Perfect fit for glass sink installations, ensuring durability and efficiency.

Stylish and Functional Drainage
