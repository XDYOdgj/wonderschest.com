Nuvo 60/917 Crispo Round Wall Light

Elevate the look of your space with the Nuvo 60/917 Crispo Round Wall Light. This modern wall light design blends elegance and functionality, creating a warm and inviting ambiance in any room.

Featuring a 14-inch white acrylic shade, this flush mount sconce is crafted to diffuse light beautifully, making it perfect for entryways, hallways, and bedrooms. Its minimalist approach ensures it complements a wide range of interior styles, from contemporary to transitional.

The Nuvo 60/917 Crispo is equipped with two 18W GU24 base bulbs, delivering optimal brightness without compromising on energy efficiency. It’s also designed with easy installation in mind, ensuring you can quickly add this stylish lighting to your home decorator plan.
