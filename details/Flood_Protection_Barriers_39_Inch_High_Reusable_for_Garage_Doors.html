Flood Protection Barriers

Our flood protection barriers are crafted from durable thickened aluminum alloy, providing unmatched strength and longevity to handle intense water pressure. Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, these barriers come with a built-in 20cm cushion that ensures a perfect fit between walls, eliminating the need for additional adjustments. With their modular panel system, you can effortlessly assemble and disassemble the barriers, making them perfect for emergency situations that require quick setup. The sealed rubber strips between panels and the floor offer a tight, water-proof seal, significantly reducing the risk of leaks and water damage. Plus, a black and yellow hazard warning tape is included to enhance visibility and safety, especially during nighttime or low-light conditions.

Key Features
  • Durable Thickened Aluminum Alloy Construction
  • Easy To Install With Included Screws and Wrench
  • Modular Design for Flexible Partitioning
  • Sealed Rubber Strips for Excellent Waterproofing
  • Hazard Warning Safety Stripe for Night Visibility

These barriers are ideal for flood prevention in residential and commercial settings, offering a dependable solution for garage doors, basements, parking lots, and more. They are particularly useful during hurricane season or heavy rainfall, acting as a crucial defense against water ingress. The detachable design allows for easy repositioning and portability, while the included connecting post ensures stability when assembling longer sections. Because of their reusability and modular build, these barriers are a smart and efficient investment for long-term disaster preparedness, helping you protect your home or business with confidence.
