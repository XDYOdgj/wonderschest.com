Upgrade Your Lighting with the VST 6 Pack LED Aluminum U-Shape Channel System

Combine practicality with style with this professional-grade LED lighting solution. Perfect for both residential and commercial use, this versatile channel system is designed to enhance your space with bright, even illumination.

Built for Performance and Style

Featuring high-quality anodized aluminum, the VST 6 Pack is engineered for exceptional durability and heat dissipation. Its sleek, modern design ensures it looks great in any setting, whether you're updating your kitchen under-cabinet lighting or adding a refined look to your living room decor.

Easy to Install and Customize

Each pack includes all the essential components, such as clear PC diffusers, end caps with and without holes, and mounting accessories. This makes installation quick and straightforward, saving you time and effort. Plus, the system is easy to cut with a fine-tooth hacksaw or miter saw, allowing for a tailored fit to your specific lighting needs.

Versatile Fit

This LED strip diffuser system works with 3528, 2825, 5050, and 5630 LED strips up to 13mm wide. The adaptable design makes it suitable for a wide range of applications, from accent lighting to decorative displays at home or during special events.

Stable and Secure

Constructed with durable anodized aluminum, the channels are strong and long-lasting. Robust metal brackets keep the system stable, allowing for secure surface or recessed mounting in any installation setting.

Perfect for Every Project

Whether you're a DIY enthusiast looking to complete your bedroom lighting project or need a reliable system for festive decorations, the VST 6 Pack offers a seamless and stylish way to transform your space. It's an excellent choice for any lighting setup that demands both performance and elegance.

  • 6-piece set includes black U-shape aluminum channels and clear PC diffusers
