Blissful Diary 50x50 Baby Play Mat

This baby play mat is specially designed for comfort and safety, offering 30% thicker padding than standard options. It provides the perfect cushion for your little one as they explore new milestones like rolling, crawling, and walking. The soft yet durable construction features padding foam and polyester fiber for a gentle touch and long-lasting use.

With a non-slip back, this mat stays firmly in place on any floor, giving you peace of mind during playtime. Its elegant tropical leaf design in earth tones adds a calming aesthetic to your nursery or playroom while offering visual stimulation that supports your baby's early development.

The mat is vacuum-packed for safe delivery and expands fully within 24-72 hours. For easy maintenance, it's machine washable, though spot cleaning is recommended to keep its quality intact. Ideal for home use with playpens or kid tents, this mat is a stylish and functional addition to any baby's space.
