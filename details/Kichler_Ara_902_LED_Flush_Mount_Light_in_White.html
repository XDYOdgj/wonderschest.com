Kichler Ara LED Flush Mount Light

This flush mount light is designed to combine elegance with efficiency, making it a perfect choice for contemporary homes. Its integrated LED with edge-lit technology ensures a soft, diffused glow across your space, eliminating harsh shadows and hot spots for a more comfortable and inviting ambiance.

With a dimmable range of 10% to 100%, you have complete control over brightness to adapt to different moods or tasks. Whether you're reading, relaxing, or simply enjoying the aesthetics, this light offers the flexibility you need in one sleek, modern package.

Crafted in a round shape and finished in classic white, the Ara light seamlessly blends into diverse interior styles. Its compact size and shallow extension make it ideal for close-to-ceiling installations, enhancing the visual appeal of any room without dominating it. From bedrooms to hallways, laundry rooms to mud rooms, this versatile light is perfect for a wide range of applications.

Engineered for energy efficiency, the LED technology not only reduces power consumption but also extends the lifespan of the fixture. This makes it a smart, sustainable choice for your home, offering long-term value and consistent performance. The easy push-on/pull-off design allows for simple and quick installation, making it compatible with standard 4" junction boxes, including pancake boxes.

Designed to thrive in damp indoor locations, this fixture is built to deliver reliable lighting in areas like bathrooms or laundry rooms. The combination of modern minimal design and durable construction ensures both style and function, making it an ideal lighting solution for your home's interior.

Key Features:

  • Integrated LED with edge-lit technology for even light distribution
  • 1100 lumens brightness with dimmable option (10% to 100%)
  • Modern minimalist design with sleek round flush mount
  • Ideal for damp indoor locations and main lighting needs
  • Easy push-on/pull-off installation for 4" junction boxes
