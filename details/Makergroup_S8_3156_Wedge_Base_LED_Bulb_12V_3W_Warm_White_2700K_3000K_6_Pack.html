Makergroup S8 3156 Wedge Base LED Bulb

Elevate your outdoor lighting with the Makergroup S8 3156 wedge base LED bulb. This energy-efficient solution combines modern technology with classic design to create a beautiful and functional lighting option for your garden and landscape.

Efficient Brightness, Beautiful Light

The 3W bulb delivers 300 lumens of light output, ensuring bright illumination without excessive energy consumption. This makes it an excellent choice for those looking to reduce electricity costs while still achieving a warm white glow that closely resembles traditional halogen lighting.

Quality and Durability

With a 35,000-hour lifespan and a 2-year warranty, these bulbs are built to last and offer long-term value. The high color rendering index (CRI) of 82 ensures accurate and natural color representation, making your outdoor spaces look their best at all times.

Easy Installation and Versatility

Designed for existing wedge base sockets, these bulbs are simple to install. Additionally, their wide input voltage range of AC10-18V/DC10-30V provides compatibility with a variety of low-voltage systems, reducing the need for multiple transformers and making them a versatile solution for any outdoor lighting setup.

Perfect for Outdoor Spaces

Ideal for use in pathlights, garden lights, and landscape lighting projects, the Makergroup S8 3156 LED bulb brings a safe and stylish light to your driveway, steps, or outdoor seating area. Its consistent warm white color remains unchanged over time, making it an excellent choice for ambient and long-term lighting applications.
