Transform Your Space with VANoopee 5-Color Dimmable LED Track Light Heads

Elevate your interior with modern, customizable lighting. These LED track lighting heads are designed to adapt to your needs with five different color temperature settings, ranging from warm white (2000K) to bright daylight (6000K). Whether you're looking for a cozy atmosphere or a vibrant, dynamic look, these lights can change to suit your mood and environment.

Versatile Lighting for Every Room

With 10%-100% dimmability and compatibility with TRIAC dimmers, you can adjust light intensity to match your activities. This makes them ideal for kitchens, art galleries, or any space that requires both function and style. Their flicker-free operation and CRI90+ rating ensure that colors appear vibrant and natural, making the lighting more engaging and visually appealing.

Energy-Efficient & Durable Design

These track lighting heads are built to last, offering durable construction suitable for both residential and commercial settings. Each 10W unit produces 800 lumens with a focused 24° beam angle, delivering bright and even illumination equivalent to a 60W halogen bulb. Their H-type design ensures a perfect fit in existing H-type track lighting systems for seamless installation.

Why Choose VANoopee?

  • 5 Color Temperature Options: From warm white (2000K) to cool daylight (6000K), customize the ambiance to your preference.
  • Dimmable LED Lights: Adjust brightness with a TRIAC dimmer for flexible, energy-efficient lighting.
  • Flicker-Free & CRI90+: Enjoy precise color reproduction without interruption for a more immersive lighting experience.
  • 24° Beam Angle & 800lm Brightness: Achieve focused and bright illumination that rivals traditional halogen lighting.
