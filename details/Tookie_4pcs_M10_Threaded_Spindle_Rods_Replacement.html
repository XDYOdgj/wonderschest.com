Tookie 4pcs M10 Threaded Spindle Rods Replacement

Ensure the safety and durability of your pressure-mounted baby gate with our high-quality spindle rod replacement. Designed for easy installation and long-lasting use, these rods are built to fit seamlessly with your existing gate structure.

Key Features

  • Durable and Strong Construction: Made with a combination of metal and PVC, these spindle rods offer superior strength and resistance to wear, making them ideal for daily use.
  • No Tools Required: Effortless installation without the need for additional tools or damaging the walls, ensuring a safe and hassle-free replacement process.
  • Universal Fit: Compatible with most pressure-mounted baby gates featuring M10 spindle holes, including banister, pet, stair, and walk-through gates.
  • Complete Replacement Set: Includes 4 pieces, so you can easily replace damaged parts and keep your gate secure and functional.
  • Perfect for Home Safety: An essential accessory for parents and pet owners looking to maintain a safe environment for their children and animals.

Why Choose Tookie?

Our threaded spindle rods are specifically engineered to provide a solid and reliable connection, ensuring your gate remains secure even with frequent use. The included screws and nuts simplify installation, and the adjustable design allows for a snug fit in various gate setups.

Whether you're repairing a damaged gate or upgrading to a more durable one, these rods are a smart investment. They are designed to last longer, reduce the risk of accidents, and give you the peace of mind you need at home.

Always check your gate's specifications to confirm it's compatible with M10 spindle holes. With Tookie, you get a reliable, easy-to-install, and highly durable replacement that fits most pressure-mounted baby gates.
