Capture Forever Memories with Creative Roots Handprint Stone Kit

This handprint stone kit is designed to help families and pet lovers create a special keepsake that lasts a lifetime. It includes a high-quality stone mold, plaster mix, and five vibrant paint pots, allowing you to capture and preserve hand or paw prints with ease. The set also comes with protective gloves, a mask, and a brush to ensure a safe and enjoyable crafting experience for both children and adults.

Perfect for newborns or beloved pets, this DIY project transforms simple materials into a meaningful family treasure. Whether you're creating a baby keepsake or a paw print stone to remember your furry friend by, the creative elements like color mixing make it a fun and engaging activity for the whole family. The kit is user-friendly and ideal for beginners, helping you make a cherished piece of art that will be displayed in your home for years to come.
