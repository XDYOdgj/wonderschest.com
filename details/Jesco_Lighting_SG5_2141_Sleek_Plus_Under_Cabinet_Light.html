Sleek Plus Under-Cabinet Light - Modern Design for Enhanced Lighting

The Jesco Lighting SG5-21/41 Sleek Plus Under-Cabinet Light brings together style and function to elevate your kitchen lighting. With a sleek, white finish and a durable extruded aluminum housing, it seamlessly integrates into your countertop or cabinet space while offering reliable and long-lasting performance.

Efficient & Reliable Lighting

This T5 fluorescent fixture is engineered for energy efficiency, making it a smart choice for modern homes. The built-in instant-start electronic ballast ensures flicker-free operation and silent performance, creating a calm and focused environment in your workspace.

Easy Installation & Maintenance

The modular design of this under-cabinet light allows you to link up to 10 units together, providing a customized lighting setup for larger countertops or islands. Its easy-to-install mounting clips and direct connectors make setup simple, and replaceable lamps ensure hassle-free maintenance over time.

Premium Build for Longevity

Featuring a secure ribbed acrylic diffuser cover, this fixture prevents light leaks, warping, and lens deformation. This ensures consistent, bright illumination across your kitchen while preserving the fixture's integrity for years to come.

Perfect for Any Kitchen

Looking to enhance visibility and aesthetics in your kitchen? This light is ideal for under-cabinet use, delivering even, glare-free illumination that creates a clean and inviting space. Whether you're cooking, organizing, or simply working, it adds a touch of elegance to your daily routine.

Key Features

  • Fluorescent T5 lamps included for energy efficiency
  • White enamel finish and durable extruded aluminum housing
  • Built-in instant-start ballast for no flickering and silent operation
  • Replaceable lamps for easy maintenance and extended use
  • Designed for seamless linking up to 10 units
