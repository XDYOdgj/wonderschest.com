Othmro LED Chip Bulb: Bright, Efficient, and Durable

Transform your lighting with the Othmro LED chip bulb, engineered for maximum brightness and energy efficiency. These bulbs are a great choice for both indoor and outdoor lighting solutions, delivering a crisp, daylight-like glow with a 6500K color temperature. Perfect for areas where lighting needs to be both powerful and sustainable, the 9W output ensures strong illumination without excessive energy use.

Why Choose Othmro LED Chip Bulbs?

  • Super Bright 5730 LED Chip: Experience vibrant light output with the 6500K color temperature that rivals natural daylight, ideal for creating an inviting and dynamic atmosphere.
  • High Power with Low Energy Consumption: These bulbs deliver a powerful 9W output while keeping energy usage to a minimum, making them a cost-effective lighting solution for long-term use.
  • Durable Aluminum Board Construction: Built to last with robust aluminum board, these bulbs offer excellent thermal management and heat dissipation, reducing the risk of overheating and extending their lifespan.
  • Wide Voltage Range: Compatible with a 27-30V power supply, they are versatile and suitable for a variety of lighting applications and setups.
  • Perfect for High-Temperature Environments: Designed for use in surface-mounted devices and areas with high heat exposure, these bulbs maintain consistent performance over time.

Versatile Lighting Solutions

Whether you're looking to upgrade your home reading area, marine lights, or camper setup, the Othmro LED chip bulb is the perfect choice. Its 65mm diameter and 6500K color temperature make it visually striking, adding a modern touch to any lighting project. These bulbs are compatible with LED downlights, track lights, flashlights, and stage lights, offering a reliable and efficient replacement option in various applications.

To ensure optimal performance and safety, always use a constant current driver and install the bulbs on an aluminum radiator. This setup will keep your lights running bright for years, making it an ideal choice for professionals and DIY enthusiasts alike.
