TotAha 18-24 Month Sleep Sack

Gently wrap your baby in comfort with this ultra-soft wearable blanket crafted for safe, restful sleep. Designed to support natural hip development and ideal for all-season use, it’s a perfect blend of style and function for your little one.

TotAha’s sleep sack is made from our exclusive TotCare jersey knit fabric, known for its buttery softness that only improves with each wash. It provides a cozy, cloud-like feel, ensuring your baby feels secure and calm all night long.

The 1.0 TOG design offers optimal warmth, ideal for temperatures between 20-24°C. Its spacious leg opening allows free movement, promoting healthy sleep patterns and making bedtime a breeze. And with a 2-way zipper, you can easily access your baby for feedings or diaper changes without disturbing their rest.

Thoughtfully designed with chin guards and zipper guards for extra safety, this sleep sack is built to last. It has been tested for over 10,000 washes, showing no deformation or fading, giving you confidence in its durability and reliability.

Whether it's nighttime or naptimes, the TotAha sleep sack is a trusted companion that supports your baby’s growth and development. Its Boho Lion pattern adds a touch of style, making it a favorite for both boys and girls.
