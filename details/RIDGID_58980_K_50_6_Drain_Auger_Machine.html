RIDGID 58980 K-50-6 Drain Auger Machine

A dependable and efficient solution for all your drain cleaning needs, whether you're a homeowner or a professional plumber.

Powerful Performance for Tough Clogs

This drain auger machine is built with a strong 115V motor that provides the necessary power to clear stubborn blockages in drains up to 4 inches in diameter. It's designed to handle a wide range of common household and commercial drainage problems with ease and speed.

Adaptable Cables for Every Drain Size

Equipped with cables in three sizes – 5/16", 3/8", and 5/8" – this auger is ready for any pipe dimension. The adjustable feature ensures a perfect fit for your specific job, whether it's a bathroom sink, shower, or floor drain.

High RPM for Fast, Thorough Cleaning

Thanks to its 400 RPM spin speed, this machine quickly and effectively clears clogs, saving you time and effort. It's ideal for use in tight spaces where other tools might struggle to reach.

Built to Last, Designed for Control

The robust construction ensures long-term durability, while the easy-to-use cable clutch provides precise control to prevent pipe damage. This makes it a must-have tool in any plumbing toolkit for reliable drain maintenance.

RIDGID Drain Cleaner – The Ultimate Solution

Whether you're facing a minor clog or a major blockage, the RIDGID 58980 K-50-6 is your go-to tool. It's engineered for 1 1/4" to 4" drain lines, up to 100 feet in length, making it suitable for a variety of installation types. The included A-30 cable kit and A-17-A adapter ensure you're fully equipped for any task.
