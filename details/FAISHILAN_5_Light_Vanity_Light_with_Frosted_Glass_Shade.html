Elegant 5-Light Vanity Light for Modern Bathrooms

This FAISHILAN 5-light vanity light combines stunning design with superior functionality, transforming your bathroom into a serene and stylish space. Its brushed nickel finish adds a touch of sophistication, while the milky white frosted glass shade ensures even, soft lighting that enhances your beauty routine and reduces harsh glare.

Featuring a durable all-metal build, the vanity light is built to last, even in high-moisture environments. The reversible mounting allows you to adjust the light direction, making it easy to illuminate your mirror, vanity, or any corner of your bathroom with the perfect balance of brightness and ease.

Whether you're looking to upgrade your bathroom lighting or add a chic touch to your bedroom or living room, this versatile wall light is the ideal choice. With easy installation and a compact design, it's perfect for small spaces and modern interiors.
