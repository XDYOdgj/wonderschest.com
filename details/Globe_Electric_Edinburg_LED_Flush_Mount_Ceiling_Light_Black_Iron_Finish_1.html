The Globe Electric Edinburg LED Flush Mount Ceiling Light

Bring a touch of timeless elegance to your interior with this beautifully crafted ceiling light. Combining classic style with modern technology, it features a sleek black iron finish that complements both contemporary and traditional decor. The soft, diffused light from the frosted glass shade creates a welcoming and comfortable ambiance in any room.

Equipped with 18W integrated LEDs, this flush mount light delivers 1100 lumens of warm white illumination at a pleasant 3000 Kelvin. Whether you're looking for task lighting, ambient lighting, or a subtle glow, its dimmable feature allows you to customize brightness to match your mood or environment. The high 80 CRI ensures that colors appear rich and true, enhancing the visual appeal of your space.

This energy-efficient ceiling light not only helps reduce electricity costs but also supports a more sustainable lifestyle. Its long lifespan of 35,000 hours means you can enjoy dependable performance for years without the need for frequent replacements. The easy installation process includes all necessary hardware, making it a hassle-free upgrade to your lighting setup.

Whether you're updating your modern living room or adding a refined look to your hallway, the Edinburg Flush Mount is a versatile and eye-catching choice. Its shallow profile is perfect for low ceilings, while the soft and even light ensures a cozy, well-lit atmosphere. Upgrade your interior with a functional and stylish lighting solution that enhances every room with natural warmth and clarity.

  • Stylish Smithfield design with a shallow rounded body
  • Frosted glass shade for soft, even illumination
  • Energy-efficient LED with warm white 3000K light
  • Dimmable technology for customizable brightness
