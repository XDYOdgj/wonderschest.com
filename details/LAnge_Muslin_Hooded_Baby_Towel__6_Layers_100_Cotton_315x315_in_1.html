L'Ange’s Muslin Hooded Baby Towel

Wrap your little one in a cloud of softness with L'Ange's muslin hooded baby towel. Crafted from 6 layers of premium cotton, this towel combines ultra-soft comfort with breathability, making it an ideal choice for drying newborns and toddlers after bath time.

Featuring a hooded design, it provides full-body coverage and ensures a gentle, secure drying experience. The breathable fabric helps wick away moisture quickly, keeping your baby warm and dry without causing irritation. Every detail is designed with sensitivity in mind—our organic lint-reduction process guarantees a safe, hypoallergenic experience that's especially gentle on delicate skin.
