Upgrade Your Outdoor Lighting with MALORY G4 Landscape Light Bulbs

Enhance your outdoor space with these MALORY G4 LED landscape bulbs, powered by 2W output and delivering 180 lumens of bright, energy-efficient light. Perfect for accent lighting, pathways, and garden areas, their narrow 45-degree light dispersion ensures sharp, focused illumination that highlights your outdoor design with clarity and style.

Engineered for durability, these bulbs feature a waterproof construction with a silicone surface coating to withstand harsh weather conditions. They are built to endure pressure, heat, and impact, offering long-lasting performance without the hassle of frequent replacements. The high CRI over 85 and warm color temperature of 2700K create a natural, inviting glow that enhances the visual appeal of your landscape and makes your outdoor space feel cozy and welcoming.

Whether you're using them in lawns, courtyards, or pathways, these G4 LED bulbs are ideal for low-voltage lighting setups. Their JC bi-pin base ensures compatibility with most outdoor lighting fixtures, making them an excellent upgrade for existing systems. With a 4-pack, you'll find them easy to install and replace halogen bulbs, providing a cost-effective and reliable lighting solution.

These bulbs are designed for a long lifespan of up to 50,000 hours, making them a smart investment for outdoor lighting. Their energy efficiency and ease of use offer significant savings on both electricity and maintenance over time, helping you enjoy bright, beautiful landscapes without the burden of frequent upkeep.

Why Choose MALORY G4 Landscape Light Bulbs?

Energy Efficient: Each bulb uses only 2W of power to produce 180 lumens, making them a sustainable and cost-effective choice for outdoor lighting.

High Brightness: Delivering clear, focused illumination, they are ideal for accent lighting and creating a safe, well-lit outdoor environment.

Weather-Resistant: With a waterproof build and silicone coating, they are built to last in all seasons and weather conditions.

Durable & Long-Lasting: Featuring a long lifespan of up to 50,000 hours, they are engineered to withstand the elements and provide reliable lighting for years.

Warm & Natural Light: Their color temperature of 2700K and high CRI produce a warm, inviting glow that enhances the beauty of your outdoor space.

Easy Installation: Compatible with most lighting fixtures and designed for effortless replacement of traditional halogen bulbs, they are perfect for DIY or professional installations alike.
