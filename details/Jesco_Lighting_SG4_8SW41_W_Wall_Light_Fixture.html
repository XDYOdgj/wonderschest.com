Sleek Wall Light - Energy Efficient T4 Fluorescent Fixture with White Enamel Finish

Add a touch of modern elegance to your kitchen or bathroom with this stylish and efficient wall light. Designed for both residential and commercial settings, it blends functionality with a clean, minimalist look. The 8-watt T4 fluorescent lamps deliver bright, even illumination while consuming less energy and lasting up to 16,000 hours. This makes it an excellent choice for reducing maintenance and energy bills over time.

The white finish with a durable enamel coating offers a timeless aesthetic that complements any interior design. Its compact size and robust extruded aluminum housing ensure long-lasting performance and a polished appearance. Featuring a built-in instant-start ballast, this fixture operates silently and without flicker, providing a seamless lighting experience.

Whether you're looking to brighten countertops, mirrors, or office workspaces, its versatile design allows for flexible installation. With the ability to link up to 10 units, it's perfect for larger areas or adding a cohesive lighting scheme. The ribbed acrylic diffuser ensures a soft and even glow, eliminating harsh shadows and light distortion.

Easy to install and maintain, this under cabinet light is ideal for those who want reliable, modern lighting that enhances both the appearance and performance of their space. Choose this fixture to illuminate your home with efficiency and style.
